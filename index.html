Meet Iná Nakao

CERAMIC ARTIST, GUIDED BY COLOR AND INTUITION

Growing up between Brazilian and Japanese cultures informs my use of color, pattern, and rhythm, alongside influences from natural forms and systems.

I work with stoneware clay, creating hand-built forms that explore touch, function, and material.

Organic shapes and natural asymmetry guide my process. Through my work, I aim to evoke memories and create a quiet, sensory connection with the holder.
